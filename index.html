About Virat Crane Industries Ltd.

Freshness You Can Taste, Quality You Can Trust

Since 1987, Virat Crane Industries Ltd. is a trusted and leading name in the dairy industry. We specialize in manufacturing high-quality dairy products, renowned for its commitment to purity, taste, and quality. Our flagship product, Durga Ghee, is made from 100% pure cow milk, ensuring a rich aroma, authentic flavor, and high nutritional value. Alongside Durga Milk and Durga Curd, our dairy products are processed under strict hygiene standards, free from preservatives and adulteration. Whether for traditional cooking, Ayurvedic benefits, or daily nutrition, Durga Ghee is trusted by millions for its premium quality and purity.

Hygienic & Secure Storage

Our advanced storage facilities keep Durga Ghee pure, fresh, and safe from contamination.
